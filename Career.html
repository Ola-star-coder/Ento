Artificial Intelligence

Shaping the future of AI with clarity, openness, and control.

Ento is all in on building the future of AI through Aura — our intelligent software platform — and Sentinent, our next-generation robotics. Backed by Ento Cortex, our open research initiative, we’re creating advanced models that power everything from natural dialogue to complex problem-solving and intelligent automation. Join us to help shape how AI transforms industries like education, healthcare, finance, cybersecurity, and more — openly, responsibly, and at global scale.

Robotics

Ento Robotics: Bringing artificial intelligence into the real world.

At Ento Robotics, we’re building intelligent machines that think, move, and adapt to the real world. From advanced manipulation and autonomous mobility to embedded AI reasoning, our systems bring the power of Aura into physical form. Sentinent, our flagship humanoid platform, is just the beginning — designed to operate in dynamic environments, support high-impact workflows, and push the boundaries of human-machine collaboration.

Cloud infrastructure

Where AI, performance, and infrastructure converge.

Powering intelligent systems at scale starts with secure, reliable infrastructure. At Ento Cloud, you’ll help design and manage the systems that keep our global AI network running smoothly. You’ll build scalable platforms, optimize performance, and ensure our data, hardware, and networks are always available, fast, and protected. Join the team that makes Aura, Sentinent, and every Ento product possible — from the cloud up.

Ento AI Brain: Bringing Robots to Life

Ento is pushing the boundaries of innovation with our AI Brain prototype—a breakthrough that brings robots closer to true intelligence and emotion. By fusing advanced neural networks with emotional understanding, Ento’s AI Brain enables machines to perceive, learn, and interact in ways that feel remarkably human. While still a prototype, this technology marks a bold step toward a future where robots can truly connect with people and the world around them.

Aura Ai: Effortless Intelligence

Introducing Aura Ai, the next evolution in personal and professional productivity. Unlike typical assistants, Aura Ai benchmarks itself against leading AIs like Gemini and DeepSeek, delivering smarter, more intuitive support for your daily life and work. Whether you need quick answers, creative inspiration, or seamless task automation, Aura Ai adapts to your needs—empowering you to achieve more every day. Get ready for the official launch this July and experience the future of AI assistance with Ento.

Clara: The Future of Humanoid Robotics
